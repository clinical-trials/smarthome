# Commercial Launch and Social Enterprise Repositioning

## Goal

Make the website lead with the strongest initial commercial opportunity while clearly explaining that senior service is the company's social entrepreneurship mission.

## Commercial Engine

The Priority Climate Concierge package is the primary growth offer. Initial marketing and route development will concentrate on Santa Clara County neighborhoods with the strongest overlap of:

- High homeownership and detached single-family housing.
- Older housing stock that is likely to have conventional thermostat replacements.
- High household income and willingness to pay for completed-for-you service.
- Long commutes, demanding schedules, and clear convenience value.
- Route density sufficient to support multiple appointments per day.

The first three commercial clusters are:

1. Almaden, Saratoga, and Los Gatos.
2. Evergreen, Berryessa, and East Foothills.
3. Cambrian, Willow Glen, Cupertino, and west Sunnyvale.

Morgan Hill and Gilroy are a later route-extension cluster because demand is attractive but travel distance can reduce appointment density.

## Social Entrepreneurship Model

Senior work is not the primary commercial targeting strategy. It is the social mission of the business. Revenue and operating efficiency from full-price Priority Concierge appointments help sustain:

- The Senior Community Rate.
- Patient installation and app instruction.
- Caregiver coordination.
- Large-print personalized guides.
- Follow-up support.
- Partnerships and outreach that can widen access over time.

The website must avoid promising a fixed subsidy, donation amount, or one-for-one installation until the business has validated those economics.

## Website Changes

- Default the estimator to Priority Climate Concierge and present it first.
- Reframe the two packages as a commercial engine and a social-enterprise mission.
- Replace the broad “comfort and care overlap” rollout language with commercially targeted launch clusters.
- Add a dedicated social enterprise section immediately after the launch-zone section.
- Keep the senior offer visible, dignified, and easy to select.
- Update the business plan to distinguish commercial acquisition zones from community partnership zones.

## Verification

- Automated content tests require the phrases “commercial launch zones” and “social entrepreneurship.”
- Existing pricing, ZIP classification, estimator, Nest links, and responsive behavior remain intact.
