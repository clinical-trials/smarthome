Before the doorbell rings

A few sensible questions.

Is the thermostat really included? +

Yes. Both packages include one premium smart thermostat, such as a current Google Nest model selected for compatibility with your home.

What if I have two thermostats? +

We will gladly handle additional thermostats or zones. The online estimator adds a draft $750 per additional thermostat. We confirm the exact amount after reviewing your system.

Does every visit take exactly one hour? +

One hour is typical for a compatible single-zone installation. Complex wiring, older controls, or HVAC repairs can require more time. We explain that before any added work begins.

Can a family member help coordinate? +

Absolutely. A caregiver or family member can join the visit in person or by phone, receive the printed guide, and be included in follow-up support.
